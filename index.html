Legal Status of Lottery Games in India

In India, lottery games are legally permitted in only a limited number of states. At present, around 13 Indian states allow the organization and participation of lottery games, while all remaining states have imposed a complete ban. Nagaland is one of these authorized states, where lottery operations have been legally conducted since 1967.

Other states where lottery activities are legally allowed include Assam, Mizoram, Kerala, Meghalaya, Manipur, Arunachal Pradesh, Goa, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Punjab, West Bengal, and Sikkim.
